About Little Lemon

Welcome to Little Lemon, a cozy family-owned restaurant nestled in the heart of the city. Established with a passion for fresh, flavorful cuisine, we bring the zest of Mediterranean flavors to your plate. Our journey began with a simple idea: to create a dining experience that feels like home, using only the finest ingredients sourced locally.

Our Story

Founded in 2015 by siblings Maria and Luca, Little Lemon started as a small kitchen experiment. What began with homemade recipes shared among friends has grown into a beloved local spot, known for its warm atmosphere and delicious dishes.

Our Values

We pride ourselves on sustainability, community, and quality. From farm-to-table ingredients to eco-friendly practices, every detail reflects our commitment to making a positive impact while serving you the best.

Our Team

Our dedicated team of chefs, servers, and staff work tirelessly to ensure every visit is memorable. Meet the faces behind the flavors that make Little Lemon special!
